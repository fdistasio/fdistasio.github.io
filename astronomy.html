Astronomy

Since I was a child, astronomy, together with computer science, was one of the sectors that interested me most. I have currently chosen to continue my studies in computer science but I will not stop cultivating my passion for the cosmos.
At the moment I own a SkyWatcher 130/900 telescope, an entry level tool but which still offers strong emotions.
Personally I also find the branch of astrophotography fascinating, however both for the expense and the time to dedicate to this I prefer to observe the sky directly from the telescope, taking some amateur photos with my smartphone, but I do not rule out updating my equipment in the future to adapt it to astrophotography
